Explore Assam

Lush tea gardens, the mighty Brahmaputra, and untamed wilderness.

Top 10 Famous Places

Discover the unique wildlife, river islands, and cultural heritage of Assam.

1. Guwahati

The gateway to Northeast India, vibrant with ancient temples and the broad Brahmaputra riverfront.

Explore Guwahati

2. Kaziranga

Home to the prehistoric One-horned Rhinoceros, a world-famous wildlife sanctuary.

Explore Kaziranga

3. Majuli

The world's largest river island, a peaceful hub of Neo-Vaishnavite culture and birdwatching.

Explore Majuli

4. Sivasagar

The historic capital of the Ahom Kingdom, filled with grand stone temples and massive tanks.

Explore Sivasagar

5. Tezpur

Assam's cultural city, overlooking the Brahmaputra with archaeological ruins and beautiful parks.

Explore Tezpur

6. Haflong

Assam's only hill station, known for its misty mountains, emerald lakes, and tribal heritage.

Explore Haflong

7. Jorhat

The "Tea Capital of the World," famed for its vast estates and colonial planters' history.

Explore Jorhat

8. Dibrugarh

A bustling city on the river banks, known as the "Tea City of India" with stunning riverside sunsets.

Explore Dibrugarh

9. Manas

A UNESCO heritage park at the Himalayan foothills, home to rare golden langurs and tigers.

Explore Manas

10. Digboi

The heritage oil city with the world's oldest operating refinery and a poignant WWII memorial.

Explore Digboi
