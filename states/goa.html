Goa

The Sunshine State - Sun, Sand, and Spirits.

Top 10 Famous Places

Experience the perfect blend of sun, sand, spice, and spirituality in India's favorite holiday destination.

1. Calangute Beach

The "Queen of Beaches" in Goa. Famous for its vibrant atmosphere and water sports.

Explore Calangute

2. Baga Beach

The party heart of Goa. Known for its nightlife, Tito's Lane, and energetic vibe.

Explore Baga

3. Anjuna Beach

Famous for its bohemian vibes and the legendary Wednesday Flea Market.

Explore Anjuna

4. Old Goa

A UNESCO World Heritage site featuring stunning colonial architecture.

Explore Old Goa

5. Panaji

The charming capital city. Explore the Latin Quarter of Fontainhas.

Explore Panaji

6. Dudhsagar Falls

One of India's tallest waterfalls, appearing like a "Sea of Milk".

Explore Dudhsagar

7. Palolem Beach

A scenic, crescent-shaped beach in South Goa known for its calm waters.

Explore Palolem

8. Vagator Beach

Dramatic cliffs and the iconic Chapora Fort with stunning sunset views.

Explore Vagator

9. Fort Aguada

A well-preserved 17th-century Portuguese fort overlooking the sea.

Explore Fort Aguada

10. Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO site holding the remains of St. Francis Xavier.

Explore Bom Jesus
