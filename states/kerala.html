Kerala

God's Own Country - Backwaters, Culture, and Greenery.

Top 10 Famous Places

Explore the tranquil backwaters, lush tea gardens, and pristine beaches of God's Own Country.

1. Munnar

Breathtaking tea plantations, rolling hills, and misty mountains. A paradise for nature lovers.

Explore Munnar

2. Alleppey

The Venice of the East. Famous for its tranquil backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Explore Alleppey

3. Kochi

A melting pot of cultures. Explore Fort Kochi, Chinese fishing nets, and colonial history.

Explore Kochi

4. Wayanad

Lush green forests, prehistoric caves, and waterfalls in the Western Ghats.

Explore Wayanad

5. Thekkady

Home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Experience boat safaris and spice tours.

Explore Thekkady

6. Varkala

Stunning cliff-side beaches and spiritual vibes overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Explore Varkala

7. Kovalam

Famous for its crescent-shaped beaches and the iconic lighthouse.

Explore Kovalam

8. Kumarakom

A serene village on Vembanad Lake, known for its bird sanctuary and resorts.

Explore Kumarakom

9. Thrissur

The cultural capital of Kerala, famous for its grand festivals and temples.

Explore Thrissur

10. Vagamon

An offbeat hill station with pine forests, meadows, and rolling green hills.

Explore Vagamon
