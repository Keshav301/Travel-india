Uttarakhand

Devbhoomi - The Land of the Gods.

Top 10 Famous Places

Explore the majestic mountains, holy rivers, and pristine beauty of Uttarakhand.

1. Rishikesh

The Yoga Capital of the World and a hub for adventure sports on the Ganges.

Explore Rishikesh

2. Haridwar

One of the holiest places in India, famous for the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti.

Explore Haridwar

3. Mussoorie

The "Queen of the Hills," offering stunning views of the Shivalik range.

Explore Mussoorie

4. Nainital

A beautiful hill station centered around the emerald Naini Lake.

Explore Nainital

5. Dehradun

The capital city nestled in the Doon Valley, Gateway to Garhwal.

Explore Dehradun

6. Auli

India's premier skiing destination with panoramic Himalayan views.

Explore Auli

7. Kedarnath

A sacred Hindu town and the site of one of the 12 Jyotirlingas.

Explore Kedarnath

8. Badrinath

Part of the Char Dham pilgrimage, dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Explore Badrinath

9. Valley of Flowers

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its meadows of endemic alpine flowers.

Explore Valley of Flowers

10. Jim Corbett

India's oldest national park, famous for its magnificent Bengal tigers.

Explore Jim Corbett
