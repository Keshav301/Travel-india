Bihar

Blissful Bihar - A Land of Ancient Wisdom and Heritage.

Top 10 Famous Places

Step into the land where history breathes, spiritualism thrives, and ancient wisdom echoes through the ages.

1. Bodh Gaya

The holiest Buddhist pilgrimage site, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

Explore Bodh Gaya

2. Patna

One of the world's oldest inhabited cities and the ancient capital of Magadha.

Explore Patna

3. Nalanda

Home to the ruins of the world's oldest university and learning center.

Explore Nalanda

4. Rajgir

Sacred to Buddhists and Jains, known for its hot springs and the Shanti Stupa.

Explore Rajgir

5. Vaishali

Known as the world's first republic and the birthplace of Lord Mahaveera.

Explore Vaishali

6. Pawapuri

A sacred site for Jains, famous for the beautiful Jalmandir in the lotus pond.

Explore Pawapuri

7. Gaya

A sacred city for Hindus, famous for Vishnupad Temple and ancestors' rituals.

Explore Gaya

8. Madhubani

The birthplace of the world-famous Mithila art form and rich folk traditions.

Explore Madhubani

9. Munger

Known for its historic fort, yoga university, and ghats on the Ganges.

Explore Munger

10. Vikramshila

A key ancient university ruin established during the Pala Empire.

Explore Vikramshila
