Overview

Dehradun, the capital of Uttarakhand, is nestled in the Doon Valley on the foothills of the Himalayas. Flanked by the Ganges on the east and the Yamuna on the west, the city is famous for its picturesque landscape and slightly milder climate. It is home to several elite institutions like the Forest Research Institute and the Indian Military Academy. Dehradun serves as a primary gateway to several popular hill stations like Mussoorie and pilgrimage sites like Haridwar and Rishikesh. Its blend of modern urban life with natural retreats like Robber's Cave makes it a unique destination.

Things To Do

  • Explore the grand Greco-Roman architecture of the Forest Research Institute (FRI).
  • Take a walk through the mysterious Robber's Cave (Gucchupani), a river cave formation.
  • Visit the Mindrolling Monastery, one of the largest Buddhist centers in India.
  • Enjoy a therapeutic dip in the sulfur springs of Sahastradhara.
  • Explore the Tapkeshwar Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located inside a natural cave.
  • Shop for local artifacts and the famous Dehraduni Basmati rice at Paltan Bazaar.

Location

Dehradun is the capital city of Uttarakhand, situated in the Doon Valley in the Himalayan foothills.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from March to June and September to November. The weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor exploration.

Top Attractions

Forest Research Institute Robber's Cave Mindrolling Monastery Sahastradhara Tapkeshwar Temple

How To Reach

By Air: Jolly Grant Airport is located on the outskirts of the city (25 km).

By Train: Dehradun Railway Station is a major junction, well-linked to Delhi, Mumbai, and Kolkata.

By Road: Excellent road connectivity; frequent buses and taxis from Delhi (approx. 5-6 hours).

Travel Tips

- Dehradun is more urban than other hill stations, so expect some city traffic.
- Carry an umbrella or raincoat as sudden showers are common in the valley.
- Use local e-rickshaws for short distances within the city.
- Try the local bakery items, especially the famous rusk and cookies.
