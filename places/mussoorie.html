Overview

Mussoorie, popularly known as the "Queen of the Hills," is a charming hill station nestled at the foothills of the Garhwal Himalayan range. Elevated at 2,000 meters, it offers a cool climate and breathtaking views of the Shivalik range and the Doon Valley. Its colonial heritage is reflected in its architecture, cozy cafes, and the famous Mall Road. Whether it's the mist-covered peaks, cascading waterfalls like Kempty Falls, or the peaceful walks on Camel's Back Road, Mussoorie remains a favorite getaway for families and couples alike.

Things To Do

  • Take a leisurely stroll on the Mall Road and Camel's Back Road.
  • Enjoy a dip in the freezing waters of Kempty Falls.
  • Ride the cable car to Gun Hill, the second highest point in Mussoorie.
  • Explore the Cloud's End, a peaceful stretch of thick oak and deodar forests.
  • Visit the historical Christ Church, one of the oldest in the Himalayas.
  • Browse for books and local handicrafts in Landour, the quieter side of town.

Location

Mussoorie is located in the Dehradun district of Uttarakhand, about 35 km from the capital city, Dehradun.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from April to June and September to November. Winter snow makes it magical in December and January.

Top Attractions

Mall Road Kempty Falls Gun Hill Company Garden Lal Tibba

How To Reach

By Air: Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun is the nearest (60 km).

By Train: Dehradun Railway Station is the closest railhead, well-connected to major cities.

By Road: Bus services and taxis are frequently available from Dehradun and Delhi.

Travel Tips

- Carry light woolens even in summer, as evenings can get chilly.
- Mall Road has restricted vehicle entry; be prepared to walk or take a rickshaw.
- Book your accommodation in advance during the peak summer season.
- Visit Landour for a more peaceful and authentic colonial experience.
